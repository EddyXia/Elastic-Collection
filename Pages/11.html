Name - Giorno Pose

Origin - Jojo Bizzare Adventure: Golden WInd (Chapter 444)

Date - January 1996

By - Hirohiko Araki

Giorno Giovanna had a dream of becoming a gangster after one saved his childhood from abusive father and bullying from kids. Giorno finally felt respected for the first time from that man. This is an iconic pose he strikes for the manga panel.
