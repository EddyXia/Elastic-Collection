Name - Yamcha’s Death Pose

Origin - Dragon Ball Z (Chapter 21)

Date - March 1989

By - Akira Toriyama

Yamcha thought he had defeated his foe Saibaiman, but he was caught off guard by its self-destruction. His pose after dying to a mediocre enemy became the iconic pose of weakness. This pose got refrenced as a gag in other anime and manga.
